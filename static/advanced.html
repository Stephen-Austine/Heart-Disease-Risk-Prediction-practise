Model Retraining
How retraining works:
  • Every prediction you run is automatically saved to data/new_uci.csv or data/new_kaggle.csv
  • Clicking Retrain combines the original synthetic dataset with all new predictions
  • All 3 models are re-fitted and the best one is re-selected automatically
  • Updated .pkl model files are saved to disk
  • AUC scores are updated live in the Overview tab

When to retrain: After accumulating 20+ new predictions, or whenever you notice the risk scores seem off for known clinical cases.
